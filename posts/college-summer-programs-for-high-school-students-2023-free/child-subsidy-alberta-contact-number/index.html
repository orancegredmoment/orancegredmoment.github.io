Q3: What is the Child Subsidy Alberta eligibility criteria?#

A3: To be eligible for the Child Subsidy Alberta, you must be a resident of Alberta and have children under the age of 18. You must also have a low income. The income threshold for eligibility varies depending on the number of children you have.
