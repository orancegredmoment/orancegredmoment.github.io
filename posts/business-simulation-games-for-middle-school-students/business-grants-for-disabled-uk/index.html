Government Grants:#

  • Disabled Entrepreneurs’ Fund: Designed specifically for disabled individuals who are considering starting or growing a business.
  • Access to Work Grant: Provides funding for workplace adaptations and support services to help disabled individuals stay in employment.
  • New Enterprise Allowance: Offers financial support to individuals claiming certain benefits who want to start a business.

Non-Government Grants:#

  • Leonard Cheshire Disability: Offers grants and loans to disabled individuals who want to start or grow a business.
  • Motability: Provides funding to help disabled individuals purchase vehicles that meet their needs.
  • Scope: Offers a variety of grants to disabled individuals, including funding for business startups, training, and equipment.

Additional Resources:#
