Can I Do MBA From IGNOU?

Undoubtedly, an MBA is a sought-after degree that can open doors to promising career prospects. IGNOU, as a renowned university, presents an accessible and flexible option for aspiring individuals to achieve their MBA aspirations. So, let’s explore the possibilities of pursuing an MBA from IGNOU and the steps involved:

Eligibility Criteria:

  • A Bachelor’s degree or its equivalent from a recognized university.
  • A minimum of 50% marks in the qualifying examination (45% for SC/ST/OBC candidates).
  • Work experience is not mandatory but can be advantageous.

Admission Process:

  1. Registration: Register online at the IGNOU website during the specified registration period.
  2. Entrance Exam: Appear for the IGNOU OpenMAT entrance exam, which is conducted twice a year.
  3. Selection: Based on the entrance exam score, candidates are selected for admission to the MBA program.

Course Duration and Structure:

  • The MBA program at IGNOU typically spans two years, divided into four semesters.
  • Each semester comprises six courses, including core and elective subjects.
  • Study materials, including textbooks and study guides, are provided by IGNOU.

Learning Methodology:

  • IGNOU follows a blended learning approach, combining self-study and face-to-face sessions.
  • Study centers across India offer classroom lectures, tutorials, and counseling.
  • Online resources, discussion forums, and virtual classrooms further support the learning process.

Examinations:

  • IGNOU conducts term-end examinations at designated centers twice a year.
  • Passing marks are required in each course to progress to the next semester.
